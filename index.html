Code 234 - Damaged or Missing High-Voltage Signs

Condition Code

Use Code 234 when high-voltage signs are found damaged or missing on a structure.

Crew Requirement — 2MAN

Two or more linemen are required to clear this condition in the field.

No On-Site Maintenance

This condition does not require on-site maintenance — repair crew handles it directly.

Safety Priority

Missing signs pose a direct hazard — anyone near the structure may not be warned of high voltage.
